About PMG UK

Dr. Nazia Askari Haider

Founder

Dr. Nazia Askari Haider founded PMG UK in 2016 with a vision to create a supportive community for women doctors balancing medical careers with motherhood. What began as a small Facebook group has grown into a vital network of over 26,000 members across the UK, providing peer support, mentorship, and professional development opportunities. Dr. Haider's leadership has transformed PMG UK into an essential resource addressing the unique challenges faced by women in medicine while advocating for systemic improvements in workplace culture and policy.
